# 📱 Get HR Portal notifications on your phone

Add the FGA HR Portal to your home screen so it works like an app and can send
you notifications (leave approvals, new policies, expense updates, and more) —
even when it's closed.

**It takes about 1 minute. Please follow the steps for your phone type.**

Portal link: **https://fgamhrapp.vercel.app**

---

## 🍎 iPhone / iPad

> ⚠️ You **must** use the **Safari** browser for this (it won't work in Chrome on iPhone).

1. Open **Safari** and go to **https://fgamhrapp.vercel.app**
2. Sign in with your **@fgam.org.au** Google account.
3. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen).
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add** (top right). The **FGA HR** icon now appears on your home screen.
6. **Open the app from that new icon** (not from Safari).
7. Go to **Notifications** (the bell), tap **Turn on**, and tap **Allow** when asked.

✅ Done — you'll now get HR notifications on your iPhone.

---

## 🤖 Android

> Use the **Chrome** browser (the default on most Android phones).

1. Open **Chrome** and go to **https://fgamhrapp.vercel.app**
2. Sign in with your **@fgam.org.au** Google account.
3. You may see a pop-up saying **"Add HR Portal to Home screen"** — tap it.
   - If you don't see it: tap the **⋮** menu (top right) → **Add to Home screen** (or **Install app**) → **Install**.
4. The **FGA HR** icon now appears on your home screen.
5. **Open the app from that new icon.**
6. Go to **Notifications** (the bell), tap **Turn on**, and tap **Allow** when asked.

✅ Done — you'll now get HR notifications on your Android phone.

---

### Good to know

- **Each device is separate** — if you want notifications on both your phone and laptop, do the "Turn on" step on each.
- **Not getting notifications?** Open the app, go to Notifications, and make sure it says notifications are on. Also check your phone's settings haven't blocked notifications for the app.
- **Need to turn them off?** Open the app → Notifications → **Turn off**.
