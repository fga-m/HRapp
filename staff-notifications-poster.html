FGA

Get HR notifications on your phone

Add the FGA HR Portal to your home screen — it works like an app and sends you alerts (leave, policies, expenses) even when it's closed. Takes about a minute.

Portal link https://fgamhrapp.vercel.app

🍎 iPhone & iPad

Use the Safari browser

⚠️ Must be Safari — this won't work in Chrome on iPhone.
  1. Open Safari and go to the link above.
  2. Sign in with your @fgam.org.au account.
  3. Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen.
  4. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add — the FGA HR icon appears on your home screen.
  6. Open the app from that new icon (not from Safari).

🤖 Android

Use the Chrome browser

Chrome is the default browser on most Android phones.
  1. Open Chrome and go to the link above.
  2. Sign in with your @fgam.org.au account.
  3. Tap the “Add to Home screen” pop-up if it appears.
  4. No pop-up? Tap the ⋮ menu → Install app / Add to Home screen.
  5. Tap Install — the FGA HR icon appears on your home screen.
  6. Open the app from that new icon.
🔔

Last step — turn on notifications

In the app, tap Notifications (the bell) → Turn on → Allow. That's it!

Each device is separate — repeat the last step on any other phone or laptop you use.
